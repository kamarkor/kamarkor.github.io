About me

Hello! I am Kamar, a data scientist at EDF, one of the world’s largest energy companies. I am currently dedicated to developing impactful solutions in NLP and Generative AI, combining technical expertise with project management to navigate complex challenges.

Outside the office, I am a traveler. My journeys have not only shaped my perspective on the world but also fueled my curiosity for new experiences and cultures. Every new place is a chance to learn, grow, and see the world through a different lens.

Speaking of lenses, I am also a photographer. I find beauty in the fleeting moments, my main focus is street and portrait photography. It is about capturing essence and telling a story, often from unexpected angles.

When I am not working or exploring, you will likely find me on the tennis court, where I have learned that discipline, endurance, and a competitive spirit can be just as valuable in the office as they are in a match. Curious? Let’s connect and see where our passions collide, whether it is in AI, travel, or photography!
